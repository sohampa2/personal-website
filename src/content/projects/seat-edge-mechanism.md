body: "Designed an adjustable seat edge extension for 5th–95th percentile users under a hard time constraint — no custom machining, no ordered parts. Repurposed a suitcase handle as the sliding extension rail. The critical challenge was the clamp: had to hold 250 lb during seated adjustments while clamping onto 80/20 aluminium extrusion. Printed in a specific orientation to maximise layer strength along the load path. Extreme surface finish was required on the bore to grip the rod securely without slip."
