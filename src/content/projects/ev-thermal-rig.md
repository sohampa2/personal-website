title: EV Battery Thermal Test Rig
phase: PROBLEM → PROTOTYPE
body: A benchtop fixture to characterise heat rejection in 18650 lithium-ion cell packs under controlled constant-current discharge cycles. The water-cooled aluminium cold plate was machined on a Haas VF-2, and cell temperature, coolant inlet/outlet temps, and current draw are logged simultaneously via LabVIEW at 10 Hz. Data fed directly into a MATLAB thermal model calibration pipeline.
