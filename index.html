●PROBLEM→PROTOTYPE

EV Battery Thermal Test Rig

Designed and built a benchtop fixture to characterise heat rejection in 18650 lithium-ion cell packs under controlled constant-current discharge cycles. The water-cooled aluminium cold plate was machined on a Haas VF-2, and cell temperature, coolant inlet/outlet temps, and current draw are logged simultaneously via LabVIEW at 10 Hz. Data fed directly into a MATLAB thermal model calibration pipeline.

SolidWorks Haas CNC LabVIEW MATLAB Thermal FEA
●PROBLEM→PROTOTYPE

High-Cycle Fatigue Test Fixture

Designed a modular four-point bending fixture for testing CFRP laminate coupons up to 10⁷ cycles on a servo-hydraulic Instron frame. Fixture geometry allows quick coupon swap (<90 s) without re-zeroing load cell. Reduced per-sample test setup time by 65% vs. the previous welded-steel jig, enabling a full DOE matrix to complete within a single lab semester.
